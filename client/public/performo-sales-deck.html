Most SMEs are running blind — tracking performance in spreadsheets, chasing updates in meetings, and reacting to problems too late.

Disconnected Data

KPI Builder & Management

Module 01
Define the metrics that matter, track actuals against targets monthly, and surface RAG status automatically — no spreadsheets required.

What You Can Do

  • Build a KPI library aligned to strategic goals and departments
  • Log actual values monthly with commentary and status flags
  • View trend charts, % of target, and RAG (Red/Amber/Green) health
  • Assign KPI ownership to individuals or departments
  • Filter and slice performance by department, period, or category

Business Impact

  • Every leader knows their targets and sees real-time progress
  • Monthly reviews shift from data-gathering to decision-making
  • Early warning on underperforming metrics before they become crises
  • Aligns daily team output to company-level OKRs and goals
  • Eliminates manual reporting — data is always current and centralized
